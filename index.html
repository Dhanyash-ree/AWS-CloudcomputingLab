Computer Science & Engineering

Department Overview

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering was started in the year 2007 with a student intake of 60 to cater to the needs of software industries. Further, the intake was increased to 120 in the year 2009 and subsequently to 180, and 240 in the year 2014 and 2022 respectively. The Program Computer Science & Engineering is also accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) for a period of 3 years up to 2026.

Cloud Association (CSE)

CLOUDS (Computer Legends Of the Unified Department @ Sahyadri) is the official student association of the Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) Department at Sahyadri College of Engineering & Management, Adyar, Mangaluru. The association promotes technical excellence, leadership, innovation, and teamwork by organizing a wide range of academic, technical, cultural, and sports activities throughout the academic year.

📘 Technical Events

Conducts coding competitions, speed typing contests, hackathons, technical workshops, seminars, and skill development programs to enhance students' technical expertise.

🎭 Cultural Activities

Organizes dance performances, variety shows, creative competitions, and the department's annual techno-cultural fest ARPAN, encouraging students to showcase their talents.

🏏 Sports & Leadership

Hosts intra-department sports events such as the Computer Science Premier League (CSPL) and conducts the annual Aarambh investiture ceremony to welcome the new student leadership team.
